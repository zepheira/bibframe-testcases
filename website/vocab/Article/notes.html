CreativeWork

Refinements of CreativeWorks are derived from a combination of LoC 'Classes and captions for resource types (11/28/11)' and subclasses of Schema.org CreativeWork.

CreativeWork.creator

Refinement relationships (author, illustrator, sculptor, etc.) are defined as subproperties of this property. As a start, I suggest we focus on a subset of the MARC relator terms what might be applicable to Works. Cardinatlity and manadatory constraints are overlayed as a community defined profile of this model.

CreativeWork.relatedTo

Refinement relationships (hasTranslation, isBasedOn, etc.) are defined as subproperties of this property.
