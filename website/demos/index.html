Demonstrations

The following demonstration and supporting tools are used to help define and explore the MARCR data model to support the requirements of a flexible and extensible bibliographic framework.

  • A linked data navigator built from Exhibit 3.0 demonstrating a sample of 2K MARC records that have been translated via the MARCR pipeline
  • A Gadget inspector over various XML MARC record collections designed to provide a condensed summary of this data and identify patterns of MARC tag usage.

MARCR.org is a joint effort of US Library of Congress, Zepheira and you!
